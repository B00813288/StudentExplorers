Road Trip in Provence, Southern France

Recommended Duration for This Road Trip

To fully explore, it takes about 6 days.

In Provence, there is much more to see than beaches! Discover the Colorado Provençal, an unmissable spot that looks like an American desert or the vast expanses of lavender fields for sublime photos made in Provence! Feel free to extend your stay in the PACA region along the edge of the Mediterranean to discover the cities of Marseille, Cannes, Nice, Menton, Antibes…

You will like:

  • The burning sun of Provence
  • The refreshing Waterfalls
  • The colorful Landscapes

A Sample of your Itinerary on the Roads of Provence:

  • The Abbey of Notre-Dame-de-Senanque
  • The Town of Gordes
  • The Path of the Ochres
  • The Colorado Provençal
  • The Vallon des Carmes
