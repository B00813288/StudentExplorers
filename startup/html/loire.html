Road Trip Through the Châteaux of the Loire

Recommended Duration for This Road Trip :

To fully explore, it takes about 5 days.

Did you know that the Loire has no less than 3,000 castles? It would take you more than a year to visit them all! Suffice it to say that for a road trip of a few days, you will have enough to do! On the program of your stay: visits to castles and walks in the most beautiful villages of France!

You will like:

  • Historical villages
  • The breathtaking castles
  • The surrounding nature

A Sample of Your Road Trip Through the Châteaux de la Loire in France:

  • The Castle of Chenonceau
  • The Castle of Villandry
  • The Castle of Saumur
  • The City of Tours
  • The Village of Chaumont sur Loire
